Mona Books is all about finding the next great read. Whether it’s a book of Fiction or Memoir, great stories are everywhere if people just take the time to look.

Established in 2022, Mona Books opened its first bookstore branch in Rockwell. Known for its comprehensive selection of books under every interest, the Philippine bookstore’s signature ambiance has also become a haven for thought tinkerers and readers alike. With over a hundred thousand titles across multiple genres, Mona Books has captured the loyalty of book lovers of all ages. From Graphic Novels to books on Cookery, Architecture and Design, Mona Books with an open mind and a thirst for a good read will ultimately find a story to fall in love with.
